Membership contributes to the running costs of all sorts of things that the group offer. Also, you must be a member of the society to stand for executive committee positions and to vote at General Meetings.

Membership is currently set at $10 per year for an individual. Membership currently lasts from the time of payment until the next Annual General Meeting. Pro rata subscriptions are not offered. Signing the form/Payment of membership fees

Please download the membership form (hosted on github.com), fill in your details and return it to us (either come to a meeting or mail it to us). Check out the form for all the necessary instructions.

Be aware that if you pay online, we will not be able to get confirmation of your payment until the next fortnightly statement arrives from our bank (we cannot do online banking).
